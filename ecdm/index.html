Time Series Forecast

Prediction for future sales volume based on historical data

How is it done?

This is a time-series data based on daily volume.

It is possible to select the forecast period. The slider lets the selection of any period from the very next day to the 30th next day.

The forecast is incremental; in the sense, it is dynamic and revised based on the most recent sales counts.

The orange band represents a range of forecasts: shows the minimum, mean and the maximum of what could be expected.

The 'X' axis represents the week of the year.

Limitations ?

Currently, the forecast ignores the larger yearly seasonal sales, concentrates on the short term sales cycle. This would need to be refined.
